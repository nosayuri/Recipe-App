***Easy Apple Crisp***
-----------------
INGREDIENTS:
- 3 cooking apples, peeled, cored and thinly sliced (about 4 cups)
- 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
- 1/4 cup cold butter or margarine, cut into small pieces
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1/4 tsp. ground nutmeg
- 2 cups JET-PUFFED Miniature Marshmallows
-----------------
INSTRUCTIONS:
(Part 1)
1 - Heat oven to 350F.
2 - Place apples in 1-1/2-qt. shallow baking dish sprayed with cooking spray; top with marshmallows.
3 - Mix flour, sugar and nutmeg in medium bowl; cut in butter with pastry blender or 2 knives until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.  Sprinkle over marshmallows.
4 - Bake 35 to 40 min. or until apples are tender.  Cool slightly.

***Avocado Toast with Tomato***
-----------------
INGREDIENTS:
- 1/2 ripe avocado
- 1 slice pepper jack cheese
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 piece of whole wheat toast
- 2 slices tomato
-----------------
INSTRUCTIONS:
(Part 1)
1 - Preheat Broiler (if you're using cheese). Toast bread (toast lightly if using cheese, toast all the way if going without cheese).
2 - Add avocado to stop of toast and spread, leave chunky or spread smoother based on your preferences. Layer on sliced tomatoes, sprinkle on salt. It's ready to devour if you're going without the cheese.If using cheese layer cheese on top and broil for 3-5 minutes until cheese is melted. Be sure to keep an eye on it to avoid burning. Enjoy!
================================
***Vanilla Yogurt Mousse With Strawberry Marshmallow***
----------------- 
INGREDIENTS:
- 50 grams Fresh blueberries
- 125 grams Vanilla yoghurt
- 80 grams Strawberry flavoured mini marshmallow
- 60 milliliters Whole milk
----------------- 
INSTRUCTIONS:
(Part 1) 
1 - Place strawberry marshmallows, vanilla yoghurt, and whole milk in a pot.
2 - Heat the mixture over the slow flame until the marshmallows are melted and all the ingredients are well-combined.
3 - Remove and cool.Divide half of the fresh blueberries into two serving glasses, and slowly pour in the mousse. Cover and chill for an hour until set. Arrange the rest of the blueberries on the top of the mousse.
4 - Serve.

***Very Berry Acai Bowl***
----------------- 
INGREDIENTS:
- 7 ounces frozen acai, softened (available in the freezer section of Whole Foods and other grocery stores)
- 1 banana
- 1/4 cup fresh blackberries
- 1/4 cup granola clusters
- 1/4 cup fresh raspberries
- 1/4 cup almond or soy milk
- handful spinach or kale
- 1/4 cup fresh strawberries
----------------- 
INSTRUCTIONS:
(Part 1) 
1 - Add the acai, banana, strawberries, almond milk and spinach to a blender or food processor. Blend well.
2 - Transfer mixture to a bowl and sprinkle with toppings.

***Brown Butter Apple Crumble***
-----------------
INGREDIENTS:
- 4 apples, peeled, cored and sliced
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/4 cup light brown sugar
- 1/2 cup uncooked oats (not instant)
- pinch salt
- 1/4 cup salted butter
- 2 tbsp sugar
-----------------
INSTRUCTIONS:
(Part 1)
1 - Preheat oven to 350 degrees.In a medium bowl, combine the apples, sugar, cinnamon, cloves and salt. Toss until well-combined.
2 - Add the apples to a deep oval baking dish.
3 - Spread out into an even layer.In a small bowl, stir together the oats, flour and brown sugar. Set aside.
4 - Heat the butter in a small pan until melted. Continue swirling over a medium flame until the butter turns a golden brown color.
5 - Remove from heat and stir into the oats mixture until well combined.Sprinkle the oats mixture over the apples.Slide the baking dish into the oven and cook for 45 minutes, until golden on top and bubbling at the sides.
6 - Remove from the oven and let cool slightly before serving.

***Avocado Toast with Tomato***
-----------------
INGREDIENTS:
- 1/2 ripe avocado
- 1 slice pepper jack cheese
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 piece of whole wheat toast
- 2 slices tomato
-----------------
INSTRUCTIONS:
(Part 1)
1 - Preheat Broiler (if you're using cheese). Toast bread (toast lightly if using cheese, toast all the way if going without cheese).
2 - Add avocado to stop of toast and spread, leave chunky or spread smoother based on your preferences. Layer on sliced tomatoes, sprinkle on salt. It's ready to devour if you're going without the cheese.If using cheese layer cheese on top and broil for 3-5 minutes until cheese is melted. Be sure to keep an eye on it to avoid burning. Enjoy!
